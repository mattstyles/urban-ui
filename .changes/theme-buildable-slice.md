`@urban-ui/theme` ships the contract's buildable slice: the surface planes,
`neutral`/`accent` flavour scales and the equi-perceptual status trio, the
emphasis (`advance`/`recede`) and physical (`shade`/`tint`) ramps, and the
statics — now including `currentColor` as a contextual passthrough. Text
voices, the gap/inset trios over the shared size ramp, and the shape
vocabulary land alongside. Every group is a StyleX var group themeable per
subtree; `trace`/`glow` are pitched as washes.
